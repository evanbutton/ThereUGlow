Everything on this page is absolutely free to use, no strings attached, under the creative commons 0 license. Most of it was created entirely by us at There U Glow over the last many years as a labor of love. There are also wonderful resources from a wide variety of other places in here.

As we continue to grow it is important to us to sustainably provide a taste of the unique and wonderful space we create with the larger world. These resources are made specifically with that desire in mind.

Claire’s Intro to (Addressable) LED Strips

For Artists and Non-Technical Babes

This guide is one of the fundamental teaching tools of There U Glow and helped inspire the camps theme, foundation and goals.

Glow Club Pathlighter Badge

This open source PCB (Printed Circuit Board) was created by Darcy Neal for There U Glow to teach people about LEDs and soldering, and as a way to demystify the process creating your own custom circuit boards. It debuted at SOAK ’22 and has been iterated and used for workshops across the globe and locally ever since. We encourage you to make your own!

Open Source Sticker Files

Stickers designed by There U Glow to give out to the public, and for you to print and play with. Use these .SVG files however you like, no attribution needed.

Free Lasercutting Files

.SVG files very similar to our sticker files, but designed for lasercutters and other CNC machines like vinyl cutters.
